About the Ministry

The Delta State Ministry of Education oversees all aspects of primary and secondary education across 25 local government areas.

Our Mission

Building a World-Class Education System for Delta State

The Delta State Ministry of Education manages primary and secondary education across all 25 LGAs. We oversee 1,248 schools, 24,600 teachers and 412,000 students.

Our mandate includes teacher recruitment, posting and transfer, school construction and renovation, curriculum implementation, examination management and the digital transformation of education administration through this portal.
